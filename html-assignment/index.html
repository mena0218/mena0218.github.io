About Me

I grew up with the world of video games and the casual passion my father has towards motorsports. They've captured my imagination and still do to this day. When I look at video games, I see a canvas that I can interact with and when I watch F1, I see some of the bravest men challenge themselves against each other around some of the most beautiful circuits in the world.

"No matter how many times we fail, no matter how far we fall, in the end you are still worthy" -Finn

I may not like books, but I love stories and immersing myself in a believable living breathing world. As I grew older, my field of interest turned to psychology and video editing. I strive to always be there to help people with anything that is in my power.
